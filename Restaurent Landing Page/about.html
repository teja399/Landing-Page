About Us

Sitara Grand is a hotel and restaurant brand with multiple locations in Hyderabad, India, offering various services and cuisines. Some locations include Hotel Sitara Grand in Lakdikapul and L.B. Nagar, while the Sitara Grand restaurant in Banjara Hills is known for its North Indian, Chinese, and other cuisines.
